    Map System Tutorial
    1. Choose a filter

    Choose one of the filters on the left-hand side to see the map suggestions

    2. Adding Locations

    Select a location by clicking on a marker and click “Add To Trip” to place it on your route.

    3. Reordering Stops

    Use the ↑ and ↓ buttons to change the order of your route.

    4. Removing Locations

    Click the ✕ button to remove a stop from your list.

    5. Viewing Routes

    Your route will automatically update on the map when changes are made.

    Tip: You can rebuild the route anytime if something looks off.
